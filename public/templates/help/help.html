What is the purpose of Chainsearchers?

Chainsearchers allows people to share lists of links, and browse these lists in one window.

How do I browse a list?

Just click the "Browse" link at the top right of any list.

How can I find lists to browse?

You can filter lists by Popular or Recent at the top of the main page. You can also filter lists by any tags they may have. Soon we will implement a popular tag panel and a random list finder.
